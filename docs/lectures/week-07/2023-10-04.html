\[ I = \frac{n}{\sum_i \sum_j w_{i,j}} \frac{\sum_i \sum_j w_{i,j} z_i z_j} {\sum_i z_i^2} \] where \(n\) is the number of observations, \(z_i = y_i - \bar{y}\), and \(w_{i,j}\) is the cell corresponding to the \(i\)-th row and \(j\)-th column of the spatial weights matrix.
